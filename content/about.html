Our story (timeline)

A practical journey from the plant floor to an Africa-focused export program.

  1. Origins

    Manufacturing-first foundation

    Founded by a veteran of Egypt’s paint industry, E&W started with hands-on manufacturing experience — formulations, materials, and plant realities that determine whether a coating system performs consistently.

    • Formulation-aware technical guidance
    • Quality and consistency mindset

  2. Growth

    Raw materials & distribution network

    We expanded into supplying both raw materials and finished coatings, building active relationships across Egypt’s coatings ecosystem and learning the operational details that keep supply dependable.

    • Procurement and sourcing coordination
    • Broad view across the coatings value chain

  3. Export readiness

    Built a B2B export program

    As our network matured, we developed a focused export workflow: clear MOQs, export packing, documentation readiness, and logistics aligned upfront to reduce delays and surprises.

    • Documentation pack: TDS / COA / SDS as needed
    • Incoterms support: FOB / CFR / CIF

  4. Today

    Africa-focused alkyd resin exports

    We specialize in long-, medium-, and short-oil alkyds, including modified grades used in architectural coatings, industrial enamels, and printing inks — supplied B2B with predictable lead times and responsive coordination.

How we work

A straightforward, B2B-only process designed to keep specs and timelines clear.

  1. Requirements: application, target grade/spec, destination port, and Incoterms.
  2. Proposal: availability, MOQ, lead time, pricing, and documentation pack (TDS/COA/SDS).
  3. Execution: packing, shipment coordination, handover, and milestone updates.
