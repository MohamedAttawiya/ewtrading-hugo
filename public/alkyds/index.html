Alkyd Resin Families

Long Oil Alkyd Resins

Flexible films with extended open time for smooth finishes.

Applications: decorative enamels, wood coatings, metal primers.

Performance: good flow and penetration for uniform gloss.

Medium Oil Alkyd Resins

Balanced hardness and drying for general industrial needs.

Applications: industrial enamels, machinery coatings, maintenance systems.

Performance: controlled drying with durable films.

Short Oil Alkyd Resins

Fast-drying, high-gloss alkyd resins for metal finishes.

Applications: metal topcoats, quick-dry primers, baking enamels.

Performance: rapid hardness development and gloss retention.

Urethane-Modified Alkyd Resins

Enhanced hardness and chemical resistance for demanding service.

Applications: flooring, marine, heavy-duty protective coatings.

Performance: superior abrasion and solvent resistance.
