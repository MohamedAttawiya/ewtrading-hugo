About E&W Trading Group

We are a family-led business with 20+ years of experience in paint manufacturing, raw materials, and distribution. Today, our primary mission is clear: export high-quality Alkyd Resins across Africa, backed by deep technical know-how and an established supply network in Egypt.

Our Story

Founded by a veteran of Egypt’s paint industry, E&W grew from hands-on manufacturing expertise into a trusted supplier of both raw materials and finished coatings. Over nearly two decades we’ve worked closely with materials, formulations, and plant operations — bridging the gap between technical decisions and reliable supply.

As our network expanded, we developed a focused export program for Alkyd Resins — leveraging long-standing supplier relationships and logistics experience to serve African markets efficiently.

What Sets Us Apart

  • 20+ years across manufacturing, raw materials, and distribution
  • Practical, technical guidance on resin selection & application
  • Established sourcing & export logistics from Egypt to Africa
  • Documentation readiness: TDS → COA → (SDS where applicable)

Primary Focus: Alkyd Resin Export

We specialize in long-, medium-, and short-oil alkyds, including modified grades used in architectural coatings, industrial enamels, and printing inks. Our process is B2B only, with minimum order quantities, palletized shipments, and standard INCOTERMS (FOB/CFR/CIF).

Depth in the Value Chain

Beyond resin export, our background spans the entire coatings value chain — from raw material sourcing and plant processes to finished product distribution. This breadth helps us anticipate constraints, align specs, and keep lead times predictable for our partners.

Note: While we operate distribution channels in Egypt, this website does not offer consumer sales. We support bulk B2B supply only.

Sourcing & Relationships

We maintain active relationships across Egypt’s coatings ecosystem. We have supplied raw materials used in large-scale manufacturing and also procure finished products in bulk when projects require a combined solution. Brand ownership remains with respective manufacturers.

1) Requirements

Share application, specs/grade targets, destination port, and preferred INCOTERMS.

2) Proposal

3) Execution

We coordinate packing, shipment, and handover; status updates provided throughout.
