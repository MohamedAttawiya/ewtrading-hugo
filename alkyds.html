Alkyd Families

Long Oil Alkyd

High oil content (~56–70%). Flexible films, excellent gloss & retention. Typical uses: exterior enamels, wood, marine.

  • Exterior enamels
  • Wood varnishes
  • Marine

Medium Oil Alkyd

Balanced dry, hardness & flexibility (~46–55%). Typical uses: architectural enamels, varnishes, interior/exterior.

  • Architectural
  • General purpose
  • Varnishes

Short Oil Alkyd

Low oil (<46%). Fast-drying, hard films. Typical uses: primers, industrial enamels, corrosion-resistant systems.

  • Primers
  • Industrial
  • Fast dry

Urethane-Modified Alkyd

Alkyds with urethane groups for higher hardness, chemical resistance and durability. Typical uses: parquet varnishes, floor coatings, automotive finishes.

  • Floors
  • Automotive
  • High durability
